Idea Ideation Wireframes/Prototyping Development Final

Idea from project 1

Umail is an APP based programme that can be downloaded to any device to provide a quicker and more personal approach to Email communication. Umail gives the user the option to send voice messages either as a voice recording to send to the recipient (with a playback option before sending) or physically calling while sitting at your desk and not having to pick up your phone.

Ideation

11am Thursday, September 17th

Idea 1: Seeing as most mobile devices have a microphone, or voice recording set up. I am thinking of making a mobile phone app of the brand ‘Umail’. It will contain everything a normal emailing system would include but also incorporate voice recording emails to other Umail accounts.

Idea 2: Similar to Idea 1, making a mobile app of my brand ‘Umail’. But adding group chats and video calling.

Idea 3: A mobile app which requires a QR code to share information to one and other through the Umail app.




4pm Thursday, September 17th, 2020

From my previous ideas, I’ve combined idea 1 & 2 together and have come up with the idea of designing a mobile phone and laptop app which provides you with an emailing service through the option of voice recording your messages and typing them. It is also going to have the option to call so people can have business calls via Umail and have group chats so people can send voice recorded messages. I am going to begin this process by making prototypes of the app for both mobile and laptop devices.

Wireframes/Prototyping


Friday, September 18th

I decided to prototype my app on Figma first, I did this so I could get an idea of what I wanted my app to look like and see how many pages I would need to have. The first three images provided below are the launching page, home page and inbox page. On the homepage, there is a list of all the different selections of what Umail provides, such as inbox, group calls etc. On the second row of wireframes below I have designed an opening email page, voice recording page and typing an email page. I also made a page for searching, an account page and an alert page for when you get a new message. The last three wireframes are the group call page, group chat and call history page.

Development


Monday, 21st September

Over the weekend I started developing my app using Adobe XD, I used my wireframes from figma to help put together the different pages of the apps. I decided to stick with the colour scheme from project one as I thought the colours worked well together and are fresh and clean.


Monday, September 21st

Here I developed some website app pages on Adobe XD, I decided to not carry on with my website prototypes after this as I wanted to focus more on the mobile app. If I was to develop my website further I would’ve added more pages similar to the mobile app. For now, the mobile app was my main focus of this project.
